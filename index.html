The top five things you need to do while in Aarhus

Rent a bike!
This one is an absolute must. Aarhus is finest when seen up close and the best way to do that? Just look at all the locals! Go for a ride through the streets and stop at the interesting boutiques and monuments. Or hit the pedals up and down the hills of Skåde or around the Brabrand lake.

Get your camera out!
Don't forget to capture all of the city's beauty. Aarhus is a picturesque city! Just take a look at these photos ⬇️

Get some food!
If you're a picnic person, Aarhus is the place. Parks such as The Botanical Garden, The City Hall Park and Mindeparken definitely have picnic-potential. You can also eat at Aarhus Street Food Market, where you'll also experience the "hygge" atmosphere.

Coffee and a stroll...
A favorite thing of mine is a stroll through the streets of the lively city. Go to the Latin Quarter and grab a coffee at one of the small cafés. Walk the narrow, cobbled streets down by the harbor and along side the river that runs through the city...

Bonus tip: You can rent a kayak or canoe and sail down the river!
